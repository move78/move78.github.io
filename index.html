Future City Mapping

About Us

Tap into the underlying rhythms that run throughout your city.
Our platform brings together disparate datasets, scattered across an ever-growing number of data portals, to your fingertips. We not only eliminate the hours spent in search of finding, downloading, and reuploading datasets to data visualization softwares for expert users, but also eliminate the high financial barrier to entry into the world of geospatial data for novice users in government, research, and academia.
By bringing these portals together, Move78 will be able to power the next generation of organizations and individuals entering in the geospatial mapping area, backing nascent mapping software and projects with a comprehensive and aggregated database of government and private data, meticulously developed city-by-city.
